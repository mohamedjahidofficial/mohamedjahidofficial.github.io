Get Scanner solution

Get scanners are designed to directly enter printed (or typed) text from magazines, newspapers, catalogues, brochures, letters, memos etc. ... OCR packages are designed to read large volumes of text, Get scanners are optimized tools that read small bits of text — instantaneously and in any application.
